Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in EXCEL 5.0?

+

EXCEL 5.0 is open to all BCA and BSc (Computer Science) students.

How many members should a team have?

+

Each team must consist of 19 members.

Can multiple teams from the same college participate?

+

Yes, any number of teams from a single college can participate.

Is participation in all events mandatory?

+

Yes, participation in all events is mandatory to qualify for the overall championship.

Will lunch be provided during the event?

+

Yes, lunch will be provided to all registered participants on the event day.

Are judges' decisions final?

+

Yes, all decisions made by the judges are final and binding.

Where can participants find detailed event information?

+

Detailed information is available on the official EXCEL 5.0 website.

Whom should participants contact for event-specific rules?

+

Participants should refer to the website for event rules and instructions.
